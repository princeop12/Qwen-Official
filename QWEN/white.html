Introduction

QWEN is a decentralized cryptocurrency project built on the Solana blockchain, designed to prioritize transparency, real-world utility, and full community control. After learning from an initial presale failure, QWEN has returned with a smarter structure and a renewed commitment to fairness, accessibility, and growth without centralized control.

Vision

We envision a future where crypto belongs to the people - not to teams or whales. QWEN eliminates unfair founder allocations and gives power directly to its users through fair token distribution, utility-focused development, and on-chain governance.

Key Features

  • 50% public presale for wide distribution
  • 10% team allocation with transparency
  • Built on Solana for fast, low-cost, eco-friendly transactions
  • Backed by liquidity reserves to ensure market health
  • Community-first roadmap with DAO governance ahead

Roadmap (Q3-Q4 2025)

Q3 2025: Relaunch Presale, Smart Contract Deployment, First DEX Listings

Q4 2025: DAO Launch, Marketing Campaigns, Partnership Integrations

2026+: Ecosystem Expansion, Real-World Utility, DeFi Tools
